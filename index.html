This sketches showcase the potential features of our extension, FitCheck, including potential features such as AI generated clothing suggestions, an extension that identifies the product being looked at and offers suggestions from other stores, ability to be used on mobile devices, and a website that pulls different buying options from different websites and presents them in a list.
